The Turtle and The Rabbit

A rabbit and a turtle met at the river.
Rabbit said,"Hi, You're so slow?"
"Maybe because your feet is small."
The turtle didn't like the rabbit's joke.
The turtle wants to test the rabbit.
"If you like," turtle said, "Let's have a race."
"A race? With you? rabbit said"
"Okay,Let's meet at the river"
"That's a deal," said the turtle.
Let us meet at seven o'clock in the morning
The next day, the rabbit and turtle met
As the race began, the rabbit ran very fast
As the rabbit reached half of the way.
He looked back and saw the turtle still far away
So he decided to rest under a tree.
"Poor turtle. How can he win against me? "
"La-La-La" The rabbit sang!
He sat down, the rabbit fell asleep.
He was very confident that he can win.
Meanwhile, the turtle kept crawling...
Turtle just kept going into the race
Almost an hour had passed, The rabbit woke up.
The rabbit ran as fast as he could
until the finish line
but it was already too late.
The turtle had already won the race.
